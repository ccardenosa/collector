Collector is built on either the Debian Stretch Slim base image or the RedHat 8 Universial Base Image. The following Debian packages (or their Redhat equivalents) are installed.

ca-certificates
curl
google-perftools
kmod
libcap-ng0
libelf1
libidn11
libssl1.0.2
libuuid1
